Roobet KYC Levels (What’s Usually Asked For)

KYC Level What It Typically Requires When It’s Usually Needed
Level 0 – Basic Email verification and basic account details. Site access and small activity. Limits are conservative; features may be restricted.
Level 1 – Standard ID Government ID (passport/ID/driver’s license) + selfie/face match. Most withdrawals and higher daily/weekly limits; often requested before the first cashout.
Level 2 – Enhanced Proof of address (utility/bank statement) and, sometimes, source-of-funds/source-of-wealth documents. Large limits, unusual activity flags, or compliance reviews. Enables faster processing and higher thresholds.
Note: Exact requirements and naming may differ by jurisdiction and can change. Follow the prompts in your account’s verification area.

Tips to Avoid Delays

  1. Complete the requested KYC level before a big withdrawal.
  2. Double-check wallet addresses and use the correct network for the asset.
  3. Keep transaction IDs/tx hashes handy for support.
  4. Review bonus terms; don’t request a withdrawal while a bonus is locked or wagering is incomplete.

Payments are subject to your country’s rules and Roobet’s terms. If something looks off, contact support with your account email and transaction details.

Roobet Payments & KYC FAQ

Do I need KYC to withdraw from Roobet?

In most cases, yes—at least standard ID verification is required before the first withdrawal and for higher limits.

Which documents should I prepare?

Commonly a government ID and a live selfie/face match. For enhanced checks, proof of address and sometimes source-of-funds.

How long do crypto withdrawals take after approval?

They’re typically quick once approved; the exact time depends on internal reviews, your KYC level, and blockchain network congestion.
